Kande

Overland camp at the Kande beach.
Local village nearby. Tonga tribe. Local boys make woodcarving and paintings.
Locals grow cassava, maize, mango, papaya, avocado.
Produce bricks from clay that they get near the village.
Woodcarvings: tables, chairs, bao boards, keyrings.
Local boys hang out at the gate of the camp.

Local school has 1500 pupils. Primary education is free, but
there are some school fees (uniform?, books?).
Library has many books in English, all donated. Some textbooks in Chichewa.
The principle gives a short overview of the school but spends most
of the time talking about possilbe donation schemes.

Locals drink banana beer and smoke the local weed (Malawiwa ?).
Locals speak Tonga, Chichewa, English, some also Swahili, some also
some other local languages.
Kaarel. Kande beach, Lake Malawi, Malawi. 2009-08-30
