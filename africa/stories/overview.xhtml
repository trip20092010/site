Africa Trip. Overview

Geography

  • Total distance overland: ~18000km
  • Most Southern point: The Cape of Good Hope

Weather

  • Very little rain:
    • in the montane forest towards the Mweka Gate,
    • 1h long heavy rain in Entebbe,
    • a bit of rain in Uganda on the equator,
    • a 10-minute heavy rain on Kendwa beach, Zanzibar,
    • a couple of times in Cape Town
  • Lowest temperature: Uhuru Peak Kilimanjaro, Ngorongoro Crater rim, Uis, Cape Town
  • Highest temperature: ?

Clothing

  • Most used footwear: flip-flops, one pair broke in Malawi, so bought another pair in Zambia
  • Most underused piece of clothing: snowboarding pants: worn only for 8 hours
  • Laundry (all by hand, i.e. no washing machine): Heron Hotel, Jinja (service), Ruhengeri, Naivasha, Kendwa, lake Kariba (service), Swakopmund (service).

Health

Nobody fell ill with malaria or bilharzia (at least during the trip). Everybody experienced diarrhea. In Maun most of the people were sick, some 4 even went to the doctor. But not so seriously that activities had to be cancelled.

Things

  • Things lost: TERE-shirt, 1 head torch, 1 book (State of Africa), pair of flip-flops
  • Things gained: 2 head torches (neither worked properly), book Asimov "Foundation" (traded for a South Africa Lonely Planet previously stolen from the truck), Kenya 8th grade "Science" textbook, 2 Kiswahili phrase books, 5 obsolete Zimbabwean dollars, many coins from different countries (incl. Australia), 2 paintings, several gifts e.g. Masai and Malawi woodcarvings (later given away), pair of flip-flops
  • Things harmed: burned holes into the sleeves of a thick fleece

Other

  • Books read: State of Africa (about 10% completed), Kiterunner, Foundation (about 70%), Kenya 8th grade Science textbook (about 20%), bits of several phrase books (e.g. Kiswahili), bits of several travel books (e.g. Lonely Planet, Rough Guides)
  • Films seen: Joy Adamson Story, Hotel Rwanda, Harry Potter
