Visas

Visa regulations for Hungarians and Estonians entering East and South African countries as tourists in July–October 2009. For up-to-date information see Wikipedia: Visa requirements for Estonian citizens#Africa and Wikipedia: Visa requirements for Hungarian citizens#Africa.

Country Hungary Estonia Comment
Kenya 25 USD (border) 25 USD (border) Single entry visa allows re-entry from a member state of East African Community.
Tanzania 50 USD (border) 50 USD (border) Single entry visa allows re-entry from a member state of East African Community.
Uganda 50 USD (border) 50 USD (border) Need to buy another visa upon re-entry, even if from a member state of East African Community.
Rwanda 60 USD (border) 60 USD (border) Can (must?) apply online beforehand.
Malawi ? EUR (Berlin embassy) ? EUR (Berlin embassy)
Zambia 50 USD (border) 50 USD (border)
Botswana No visa required No visa required, as of March 2009
Namibia 45 EUR (Vienna embassy) 45 EUR (Vienna embassy) Visa expires 3 months after it has been issued.
RSA No visa required 85 CHF (Bern embassy)

East African Community = { Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda, Burundi }.
