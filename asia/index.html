Indonesia

  • Berastagi One day in the Karo Highlands around Berastagi.
  • Bukit Lawang Two days in Bukit Lawang and two days in the Gunung Leuser National Park.
  • Medan One afternoon and one morning in Medan.

Malaysia

  • Penang Four days in Georgetown and around.

Thailand

  • Thale Noi A morning in Thale Noi.
  • Bangkok About two weeks in Bangkok, before and after touring Southeast Asia.
  • Chiang Mai A week in Chiang Mai and around.

Laos

  • Vieng Phouka A 3-days/2-nights trek close to Vieng Phouka (Northern Laos) to visit (and overnight at) the local Lahu villages in the hills.
  • Mekong river journey A 2-day slow boat river journey down the Mekong river from Houayxay to Pakbeng (6h), an overnight stay in Pakbeng, and then from Pakbeng to Luang Prabang (8h).
  • Luang Prabang Three days in Luang Prabang: several temples (incl. Wat Xiang Thong), Phou Si hill, night market, National Museum, and the Kuang Si waterfalls outside of town.

Vietnam

  • Hoi An A couple of days in Hoi An, incl. an afternoon on a beach close to Hoi An, and an early morning at the Cham temples in My Son (55km from Hoi An).
  • Nha Trang An afternoon and an early morning in Nha Trang.
  • Dalat Two days in Dalat (1500m)

Cambodia

  • Angkor Three days visiting the Angkor temples close to Siem Reap.

India

  • Mumbai 4 days in Mumbai
