Brazil

  • São Paulo Weekend in São Paulo
  • Rio de Janeiro Two days in Rio de Janeiro (Corcovado, down-town, Rocinha, Vila Canoas, Pão de Açúcar, Maracanã)
  • Ouro Preto A day in Ouro Preto, Minas Gerais (churches, steep streets and staircases, churches, an old gold mine, and more churches)
  • Salvador Two and half days in Salvador
  • Amazonas 23-day river trip from Belém, Brazil to Iquitos, Peru (including stops (in Brazil) in Santarém, Alter do Chão, Tapajos national forest, Manaus, Tefé, Mamiraua reserve, Alvarães, Tabatinga/Leticia)

Peru

  • Iquitos Three days (over Christmas) in Iquitos, Peru. Visits to Belén, a manatee orphanage, and the Quistococha zoo.

Ecuador

  • Quito Five days in Quito and around (Mitad del Mundo, Pichincha, Otavalo)
  • Galapagos One week on the Galapagos Islands (Baltra, Santa Cruz, Rabida, Santiago, Bartolome)

Peru

  • Colca Canyon A 3-days/2-nights visit of the Colca river canyon near Arequipa, Peru. The visited villages in the canyon were Cosñirhua, Malata, Sangalle; and out of the canyon Chivay, Cabanaconde and Maca.
  • El Misti A 2-days/1-night trip to the top of the El Misti active volcano (5822m) near Arequipa.
  • Lake Titicaca Visits to Puno, the Uros Islands, Taquile Island, Copacabana (Bolivia), and Isla del Sol (Bolivia), around and in Lake Titicaca.

Bolivia

  • La Paz A couple of days in La Paz (during the carnival time), before and after climbing Huayna Potosi.
  • Huayna Potosi A 3-days/2-nights trip to Huayna Potosi (6088m) near La Paz.
  • Potosi Visit to the mining town of Potosi, the highest town in the world, at 4090m.
  • Salar de Uyuni A 3-days/2-nights trip from Uyuni (Bolivia) to San Pedro de Atacama (Chile), going through Salar de Uyuni, Laguna Colorada, Sol de Mañana, Laguna Verde, and other places on the Bolivian Altiplano.

Chile

  • Patagonian ferryride A 4-days ferry trip from Puerto Montt to Puerto Natales through Patagonian fjords and channels.
  • Torres del Paine 6 days (5 nights in tent) in the Torres del Paine National Park, Chile (tracks: "the tail of Q" + "the W" + "the road from Hosteria Las Torres to Laguna Amarga")
