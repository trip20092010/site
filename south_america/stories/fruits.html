Fruits in Manaus

Juices of these fruits are sold at a lunch place in down-town Manaus.

  • Limão (lemon)
  • Cupuacu'
  • Acerola
  • Caju (ate in the village from the tree) (cashew fruit)
  • Carambola (star fruit)
  • Goiaba (guava)
  • Jenipapo
  • Tapereba'
  • Manga (mango)
  • Jabuticaba
  • Amora
  • Buriti
  • Jaca
  • Graviola (cherimoya, custard apple)
  • Maracuja' (passion fruit)

  • Araça' Boi
  • Melão (watermelon)
  • Melancia (melon)
